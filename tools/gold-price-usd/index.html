    Live gold price per gram & per troy ounce (USD)

    This tool tracks the gold price today in USD using the global spot market as a baseline. You can instantly estimate gold price per gram or gold price per troy ounce, and convert the 24K spot into popular karats like 22K, 21K, and 18K. To approximate a retail checkout price, add an optional premium / making charge per unit.

    Methodology & calculation

    • We start from the 24K spot price per troy ounce, then convert to per gram using 1 oz troy = 31.1034768 g.
    • Karat estimates use: 24K price × (karat ÷ 24) (example: 21K ≈ 24K × 21/24).
    • Displayed values are estimates; retailer spreads, premiums, taxes, and fees may apply.

    Gold price FAQ (USD)

    What is the gold spot price?

    The spot price is the current market price for immediate delivery. This tool uses spot as a baseline and estimates karat prices proportionally.

    How do you calculate 21K and 18K from 24K?

    We use an estimate: 24K × (karat ÷ 24). Example: 21K ≈ 24K × (21/24).

    How many grams are in a troy ounce?

    1 troy ounce ≈ 31.1034768 grams.

    Does this include premium, tax, or making charges?

    No. Add an optional premium per unit to approximate retail prices. Taxes and fees vary by location and seller.
